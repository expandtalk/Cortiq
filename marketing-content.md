## FAQ Section

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is "Agentic Web"?**
A: The agentic web refers to the increasing portion of web traffic coming from AI agents like ChatGPT Browser, Perplexity, and Claude instead of human users.

**Q: How do you track AI agents?**
A: We analyze user agents, request patterns, and behavioral signatures to identify AI agents and track their interactions.

**Q: Is CortIQ GDPR compliant?**
A: Yes, 100%. We use server-side tracking without cookies and are PTS-approved by the Swedish Post and Telecom Authority.

**Q: Can I use CortIQ with my WordPress site?**
A: Yes! We offer a 1-click WordPress plugin with deep integration.

**Q: What about my existing Google Analytics?**
A: CortIQ complements GA4. We sync data and provide deeper insights GA4 doesn't offer.

**Q: How much data can I track?**
A: Unlimited events on Enterprise plans. Starter and Professional plans have daily limits.

**Q: What about data security?**
A: We use enterprise-grade security: HTTPS, encryption, Row Level Security, and regular security audits.

**Q: Can I integrate with my data warehouse?**
A: Yes! We support BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift, PostgreSQL, MySQL, and Databricks.
